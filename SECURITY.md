# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Do **not** open a public issue for a suspected vulnerability, exposed secret, authentication bypass, data exposure, privilege escalation, or other security concern.

Use GitHub Private Vulnerability Reporting for this repository when available. If that option is not visible, contact SaaSy Solutions through the contact method published at https://saasysolutionsllc.com and clearly state that the message is a confidential security report.

Include:

- A concise description of the vulnerability
- The affected feature or URL
- Reproduction steps or a proof of concept
- Potential impact
- Suggested remediation, when known
- Your preferred contact information

Do not access, modify, retain, or share data that does not belong to you. Avoid destructive testing, service disruption, social engineering, spam, or testing against other customers.

We will review good-faith reports and coordinate communication when appropriate. Do not publicly disclose the issue before SaaSy Solutions has had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and remediate it.
