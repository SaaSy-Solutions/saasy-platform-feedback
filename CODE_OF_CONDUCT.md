# Community Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and productive feedback community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Focus criticism on the product or behavior, not the person.
- Provide clear, constructive, and relevant information.
- Protect privacy and confidential information.
- Follow maintainer directions when an issue is moved, edited, or closed.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, doxxing, spam, deliberate disruption, or publication of sensitive information is not permitted.

Maintainers may edit or remove content, lock conversations, or restrict participation when needed to protect the community and the project. Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method at https://saasysolutionsllc.com.
